The Inheritance Cycle by Christopher Paolini

Emotional significance

I chose this book series as my greatest of all time as it was the only thing at the ripe old age of 12, that changed what I was interested in so drastically that it still holds 1st place. It not only shaped my interests it also gave me a perspective in life that I believe eventually changed who I was as a person.

The author portrayed characters so organically that I began modeling who I wanted to be on Eragon and conflict was done so naturally that I felt the Eragon's rage when events happen to cause it.
